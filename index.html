Controls

Enter The Arena

A/D move, W jump, Space jab, S smash, hold E for Pulse Shot, Shift Charge Shot, arrow keys specials, R full reset.

Move Set

  • Jab is fast and reliable for stacking percent.
  • Smash is slower but launches hard at high damage.
  • Hold Shift for 1 second to store a Charge Shot, then tap Shift to fire it later.
  • Hold E for Pulse Shot. It ramps up fast, but if you hold too long you overheat, explode, and lose Pulse Shot for 2 seconds.
  • Left or right arrow for Nova Rush, up arrow for Skybreak, and down arrow for Burst Field.
  • You can keep jumping in the air as long as you need.
  • Your attacks have a little extra reach to make confirms easier.

Rules

  • Percent increases knockback instead of reducing health.
  • Cross the blast zone and you lose a stock.
  • You and the CPU both have 4 stocks.
