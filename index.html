A browser-based platform fighter inspired by the feel of Super Smash Bros.

Nova

Damage 0%

Stocks 4

Charge Building

CPU

Volt

Damage 0%

Stocks 4

Controls

Enter The Arena

A/D move, W jump, Space jab, S smash, C shot, Shift charge, E blast, Z plus arrows for specials, R full reset.

Move Set

  • Jab is fast and reliable for stacking percent.
  • Press C to fire a fast ranged shot across the arena.
  • Smash is slower but launches hard at high damage.
  • Hold Shift for 1 second to store a Charge Shot, then tap Shift to fire it later.
  • Tap E to start Blast charging, then tap E again after 1 second to detonate it around Nova.
  • Tap Z for neutral special, Z plus left or right arrow for side special, Z plus up arrow for up special, and Z plus down arrow for down special.
  • You can keep jumping in the air as long as you need.
  • Your attacks have a little extra reach to make confirms easier.

Rules

  • Percent increases knockback instead of reducing health.
  • Cross the blast zone and you lose a stock.
  • You and the CPU both have 4 stocks.
