Everything essential about BEYBLADE X, in one page.

BEYBLADE X is the 4th generation of Beyblade: toyline, manga, anime, app, events, and global competition. This site compiles the key facts from official and high-signal references.

Fast Facts

Generation

BEYBLADE X is the 4th generation, launched in Japan on July 15, 2023.

Core Gimmick

Xtreme Dash: tops accelerate along a rail in the BEYBLADE X stadium.

Scale

Over 28 million BEYBLADE X units shipped worldwide as of February 2025.

Battle System and Parts

Part Structure

Each Bey has 3 main parts: Blade, Ratchet, and Bit.

Customization

Swapping parts changes performance; naming is based on those part names.

Match Basics

Official guide notes 4 win methods; first player to 4 points wins.

Type Logic

There are 4 types, and the Bit determines the type of a setup.

Compatibility

BEYBLADE X tops are not cross-compatible with BEYBLADE Burst.

Product Lines and Getting Started

BX / Basic Line

Mainline fundamentals and beginner-friendly releases.

UX / Unique Line

Unique performance concepts and specialized behavior.

CX / Custom Line

Newer line emphasized in 2025 with expanded customization options.

Starter Packs

Typically include 1 Beyblade + 1 launcher (e.g., Hasbro F9582 starter).

Start Dash Sets

Commonly include Beyblade, launcher, grip, and stadium for quick entry.

Age Guidance

Many Hasbro BEYBLADE X products are labeled for ages 8+.

Anime, Manga, and Digital

Manga

Serialized from May 15, 2023 (CoroCoro Comic), by Homura Kawamoto, Hikaru Muno, Posuka Demizu.

Anime

TV anime began October 6, 2023 in Japan; official news confirms later chapter updates.

U.S. Release

English dub TV premiere in the U.S. was July 13, 2024 (Disney XD/DisneyNow/Hulu).

App

Beyblade X app released May 28, 2024 with global multiplayer and top scanning features.

Games

XONE released November 14, 2024, and EVOBATTLE released November 13, 2025 (Switch/Windows).

Competitive Scene

World Championship 2025

Held October 11-12, 2025 at RED TOKYO TOWER, with global regional qualifiers.

Participation Classes

Official communications describe regular and open tournament classes by age bracket.

Broadcasting

Championship content planned for global streaming via official BEYTUBE channels.

Japan Event Structure

Takara Tomy guide references multiple tournament grades and B4 store entry points.
